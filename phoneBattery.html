How to Check Battery Health on Android

To check the battery health on your Android phone, you can use one of the following methods:

  1. Using Dialer Code:
    • Open the Phone app and dial *#*#4636#*#*.
    • In the menu that appears, select "Battery Information."
    • Here, you can see details about your battery's health, including its current capacity and status.
  2. Using Settings:
    • Open the Settings app on your Android phone.
    • Navigate to the "Battery" section. Depending on your phone model, this might be under "Device care" or "Battery & performance."
    • Look for an option like "Battery usage" or "Battery health." Some phones provide detailed information about battery health and usage.
  3. Using Third-Party Apps:
    • Download a battery health app from the Google Play Store. Some popular ones include AccuBattery and Battery Guru.
    • Install and open the app. Follow the instructions to check your battery health.
  4. Using Manufacturer's Tools:
    • Check if your phone has a manufacturer-specific app for battery diagnostics. For example, Samsung phones have the "Samsung Members" app.
    • Open the app and navigate to the battery diagnostics section.
